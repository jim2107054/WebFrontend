Welcome to Khulna University of Engineering & Technology

Khulna University of Engineering & Technology (KUET) is one of the leading public universities of Bangladesh giving special emphasis in the Engineering and Technological Education and research. KUET is well known for offering very high quality educational, research and developmental programs in the major disciplines of engineering as well as basic sciences. It has a sober objective to achieve excellence in quality education, research and progression to address the present needs of the country as well as the South-Western region to make it as the “Center of Excellence”. The main objective of the university is to produce high quality technologists and researchers to meet the growing needs of the country in the field of Engineering and Technology.
